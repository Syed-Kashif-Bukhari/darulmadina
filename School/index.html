Islamic School System

We believe that a child’s mind is just like a blank paper, whatever is written on it, stays on it. If the upbringing of a child is taken care of in the early stages, he would become a great human being in future. Dar-ul-Madinah Islamic School System has been introduced to provide essential Islamic knowledge and quality academic education in conformity with Shari’ah. Currently we have more than 11,000 students studying in 25 campuses of Dar-ul-Madinah Islamic School System.
