Events

30 Dec 2015

Teacher Training

Dar-ul-Madinah has started its winter vacation teaching training session for the academic year 2016-17. Training includes pedagogical and soft skills in order to grow up the nation pillars.

25 Nov 2015

Seed Activity

Seed activity was performed in Dar-ul-Madinah (Islamic school System), Fb Area, Bab-ul-Madinah(Karachi). Teachers told Madani children about plants and seeds. Madani children were given different kinds of seeds. They sowed them in seedbed. They learnt different things about Plants and enjoyed the activity.

22 Dec 2014

Dar-ul-Madinah students celebrate winter activity

Madani students celebrated winter activity in Dar-ul-Madinah Federal B. Area on Monday. To fill the activity with joy a winter-like environment was created at the school. Different materials were used to build a snowy field that looked exactly like the falling winter snows. Students wore warm dresses over their uniforms, beautiful caps, etc. All of them were happy and enjoyed the activity a lot.

18 Dec 2014

Yaum-e-Raza observed at Dar-ul-Madinah

Yaum-e-Raza observed at Dar-ul-Madinah Madani students observed Yaum-e-Raza in Dar-ul-Madinah Aram Bagh on Thursday, Safar-ul-Muzaffar 25, 1436. On the day Madani students acquired basic knowledge about the life of Ala Hadrat. They recited Naats, Salat-o-Salam and waved Madani flags. The day was observed during the daily schedule.
